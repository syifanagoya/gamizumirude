RAW v. Smack Down! Stats

-2 Smack Down! v. 2 RAW in Final Four (previous years: 2 v. 2 in 2003, 3 v. 1 (SD) in 2004, 2 v. 2 in 2005)

-RAW garnered 4 of the last 6 spots. Goldust broke the 2 year streak of a RAW wrestler having #29 (Goldust being “non-branded”). Randy Orton broke a 2 year streak of RAW wrestlers entering at #30.

-Since the brand split: Randy Orton (Smack Down! 2006), “The Nature Boy” Ric Flair (RAW 2005), & Bill Goldberg (RAW 2004), The Undertaker (Smack Down! 2003) have been #30.

-Entrants from Same Brand Streak? 4 (record is 5 in 2005), which by RAW starting with Michaels and ending with Benjamin.

-First 15:
RAW – 6
Smack Down! – 8
WWE Legend – 1

-Last 15
RAW – 8
Smack Down! – 6
WWE Legend – 1

RAW for two years running was the bulk of entrants in the final 15.

-Booker T.’s elimination marked the first time since the WWE United States Championship debutted that the WWE United States Champion was *NOT* the runner-up in the Royal Rumble.

-This was the second time #2 won a Royal Rumble. Mr. Vince McMahon won in 1999 at #2.

-This was the second time “Triple H” Hunter Hearst Helmsley drew #1. He previously drew #1 in 1996.

-Only 2 of “Triple H” Hunter Hearst Helmsley’s eliminations were by his hands alone.
