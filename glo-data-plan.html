Glo Nigeria is one of the communication networks with a very large data network coverage. Over the years, the Glo data plan network has been expanded to both urban and rural areas of the country. As the grandmaster of data, the network prides itself on a lot of data plans that have been of huge benefit to its users. Adopting global technological advancements, the network constantly adapts to growth in network advancements like introducing the 4G LTE network, which helps provide speedy network connectivity to users.

The network is known for giving out large amounts of data at the cheapest price than other communication networks. Giving clearer procedures of subscribing to the data plans available. Glo data plan is structured taking cognizance of large data users and individual data users, all in a bid to achieve a better data service delivery.

Glo Data Plans and Subscription Codes

There are different categories of the Glo data plan. They all have different subscription codes, prices, and duration it last before it expires. They are classified according to days, weeks, months, and night plans. Below are the three major methods to employ in subscribing to Glo data plans:

  • The Glo USSD code *777#.
  • The Glo High-Speed Internet (HSI) portal.
  • Through your bank mobile applications.

Glo data plans are the best choice for users under the Glo data network coverage as they provide more data value than other data networks.

How To Opt Out Of Glo Data Plan

Many Glo users find it hard to purchase data plans or deactivate their old ones. When an existing subscription to a data plan is about to expire, it may be renewed automatically for the same length of time at the current rate. However, this can cause frustration, especially when charged for a data plan you won’t need. To stop this, simply send CANCEL to 127, and you will successfully opt out immediately.

Can You Activate A Paused Glo Data Plan?

To reactivate a paused Glo data plan is very much possible. It only demands a few steps to take, which are:

  • Dial *777#
  • Select the Data, or press 1
  • Select Buy Data Plan or press 1
  • Select Auto-renew before moving to the next stage of selecting your preferred data plan. This will return you to having your data automatically renewed when it expires.
