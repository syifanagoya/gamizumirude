Birth Name: Maia Reficco Viqueira

Place of Birth: Boston, Massachusetts, U.S.

Date of Birth: July 14, 2000

Ethnicity: Argentinian [Spanish, possibly other]

Maia Reficco is an American-Argentinian actress and singer. She starred on the musical telenovela Kally’s Mashup, on the series Pretty Little Liars: Original Sin, and in the film Do Revenge.

Maia was born in Boston, to Argentinian parents, Katie Viqueira, a singer and singing teacher, and Ezequiel Reficco, a university professor. Her mother is director at her own Center for Vocal Art. Her brother, Joaquín Reficco Viqueira, is also a singer. Her family moved to Buenos Aires, Argentina, when she was six. She then moved to Los Angeles, California, when she was fifteen, for her singing and acting career.
